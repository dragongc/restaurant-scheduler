Home Page

  • Shows weekly schedule for the entire staff
  • Last Week Button
    • On-click, this button will retrieve the previous week’s schedule
    • This newly retrieved schedule will then replace the current schedule in the content box
  • Next Week Button
    • On-click this button will retrieve the next week’s schedule
    • The newly retrieved schedule will replace the old content
  • Server Link
    • Each server listed in the schedule will also have a link to that person’s profile

Your Schedule Page

  • Shows your weekly schedule
  • Last Week Button
    • On-click, this button will retrieve the previous week’s schedule
    • This newly retrieved schedule will then replace the current schedule in the content box
  • Next Week Button
    • On-click this button will retrieve the next week’s schedule
    • The newly retrieved schedule will replace the old content
  • Give Up link
    • On-click renders a pop-up form
    • Pop-up has a drop down to choose who you would like to offer the shift to
    • This shift trade will then appear in your shift trade requests

Announcements Page

  • This page will allow anyone to post a message to the message board
  • The text box at the bottom allows you to enter a new message
  • The Post button will save the message and add it to the message board with a stamp containing your name and the date

Shift Trades Page

  • This page will consist of 2 tabs
  • A shift trade is offered by an employee, accepted by an employee, then approved by a manager
  • Accepted Tab
    • Shows a list of all shift trades that have been offered and accepted by you and another employee
    • Shows the shift, the offered by employee, the offered to employee and the status of approval
    • Once approved the changes will appear in all schedules
  • Requested Tab
    • Shows a list of all shift trades that have only been offered, but not accepted, either by you or to you
    • Shows the shift, the offered by employee, the offered to employee and the status of acceptance
    • Shifts offered to you allow the option to accept by clicking the Accept link, or decline by clicking the Declne link

Profile Page

  • Clicking the picture area will allow you to upload a picture to your profile
  • Edit Profile Button
    • Changes the texts fields to enable editing
    • Makes Save Changes button visible
